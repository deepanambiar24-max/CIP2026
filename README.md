When I started the project, I had a few different ideas. I was a bit confused at first, but in the end, I picked one - and created DailyLog. 
A journal where you can create your daily entries, tasks for the day, and meal plans. 
It can even store your moods and creates a focus log - where you can enter what activities you did and time you took. 
The Focus Log is for people who spend time learning or creating something. Please go through it and let me know what you think!
